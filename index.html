Ziyi Zhang

I'm a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Texas A&M University, supervised by Prof. Nick Duffield. Before that, I obtained my B.Eng. degree in Electronic Information Engineering from the University of Glasgow and the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC).

My research focuses on machine learning and causality in time series data, combining theoretical foundations with real-world applications (e.g., supply chain, healthcare, recommender systems, and anomaly detection).
